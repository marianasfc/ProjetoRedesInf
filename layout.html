Abstract

This paper explores the concept of Simultaneous Wireless Information and Power Transfer (SWIPT) technology and its increasing significance in the development of sustainable and eco-friendly wireless networks. We discuss the basic principles of SWIPT and its various applications, including wireless charging, energy harvesting, and wireless communication. We also highlight the main reasons why SWIPT is important, such as energy efficiency, cost savings, environmental sustainability, flexibility, and scalability. The purpose of this paper is to provide readers with a better understanding of SWIPT technology and its potential benefits for the development of sustainable wireless networks.
